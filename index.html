Festivals

Diwali

Diwali, the Festival of Lights, is one of India's most celebrated festivals.

Holi

Holi, the Festival of Colors, is known for it's vibrant celebrations.

Eid

Eid is a significant Muslim festival marked by prayers and feasting.

Places to Visit

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an iconic symbol of love and a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Jaipur

Jaipur, often to referred as Pink City, is known for it's historic sites, including:

  • Amer Fort
  • Hawa Mahal
  • City Palace

Kerala

Kelrala is famous for it's serene backwaters and lush green landscapes.

Indian Cuisine

Butter Chicken

Butter chicken is a creamy and flaverful indian dish

Biryani

Biryani is a fragrant and arommatic rice dish with various regional variations. Notable ingredients include:

  1. Basmati rice
  2. Chicken or lamb (vegetables for a vegeterian version)
  3. Spices (cumin, coriander and garam masala)
  4. Yogurt and saffron for flavor and color

Dosa

Dosa is a South Indian crispy pancake made with fermented rice and lentil batter.
